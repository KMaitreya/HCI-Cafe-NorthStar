Let's Sip!

At Northstar, we recognize that every decision we make affects the health of our community and the environment, hence we're extremely careful about the ingredients we use. We choose locally grown, organic, and artisan produced foods not only to protect the environment and to support producers whose values are aligned with ours but also so that we are serving the most wholesome and delicious food we can.
